Get Started with Subtitles

  1. Set This Extension as Component 1 Or 2

    Click on the Activate button below the Subtitle : Reach Audience Worldwide extension and click Set as component #

  2. Locate the extension bottom of the screen

    Subtitles are ment to be at the bottom of the screen right ? Of course you can set the location wherever you like but come on !

  3. Open the Recognition App from the Live Dashboard

    Don't forget to open app from the button in Live Dashboard everytime you open your stream , otherwise it wont work

  4. Configure Your Recognition App

    Select your Language and Dialect from the boxes and tap "Start Listening" after you configure.

  5. We are set!
